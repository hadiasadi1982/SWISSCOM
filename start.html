Our Experience About Switzerland and Swisscom GrowGive Project

As Asylumist, Switzerland is really stressful. Because the asylum procedure continues too long.
But thinking Switzerland as an totally neutral country, it is a secure island in our case. And that's why we chose Switzerland to apply for Asylum.

During this long journey, keeping ourselves busy is really helpful for being social and to forget our sorrows.
In this context, Grow&Give Project is a good opportunity for us.
